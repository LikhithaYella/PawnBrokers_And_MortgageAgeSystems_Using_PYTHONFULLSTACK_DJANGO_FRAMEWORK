Mortgage brokers act as intermediaries between borrowers and lenders, helping borrowers find the best mortgage deals and lenders find suitable borrowers. They typically charge a fee for their services, but can be helpful for those who may have difficulty obtaining a mortgage on their own. Pawn brokers, on the other hand, provide short-term loans to individuals who provide collateral in the form of valuable items such as jewelry, electronics, or musical instruments. The amount of the loan is typically a percentage of the item's value, and the borrower has a set amount of time to repay the loan and retrieve their collateral.
